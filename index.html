Why Noir Brew

Obsession Brewed
Into Every Cup

We're not just another café. We're a philosophy — that coffee deserves the same reverence as fine wine, crafted from seed to sip.

🌱
Direct Trade

We visit every farm we work with - building long-term relationships that produce better coffee and better lives for growers.

🔥
Precision Roasting

Our roast master profiles each origin with obsessive care. Every bean is logged, tasted, and optimized for peak expression.

🧪
Lab Tested

Every batch is cupped and scored against SCA standards before it ever reaches your cup. Consistency is non-negotiable.

♻️
Zero Waste Goal
Spent grounds become compost. Bags are recyclable. We're on a misson to be Singapore's first carbon-neutral roastery.
