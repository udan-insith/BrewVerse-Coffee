Our Story

Born from a
Single Obsession

It started in a tiny roastery in Tiong Bahru — just a second-hand drum roaster, a notebook full of tasting scores, and an unshakeable belief that Singapore deserved better coffee.

Today, Noir Brew Co. is recognised across Southeast Asia as a benchmark for specialty coffee. Three cafés, a direct-trade sourcing program across five countries, and a team that lives and breathes the craft.

  • SCA-certified Q Graders on every roasting team
  • 12 direct-trade farm partnerships across 5 countries
  • Roasted fresh — dispatched within 48 hours of roasting
  • 200kg compost diverted from landfill every month

The Craft

From Farm to
Your Cup

Three obsessive steps, zero shortcuts. This is how great coffee is born.

01
🌿

Source

We travel to every farm personally. Cup on-site. Build relationships over years, not transactions. Only when the flavour is extraordinary do we bring it home.

02
🔥

Roast

Our roast master builds bespoke profiles for every origin — developing sugars, managing acids, coaxing out complexity. Each profile is tested over 6+ sessions before release.

03
☕

Serve

Dispatched within 48 hours of roasting to our cafés and subscribers. Brewed by baristas trained to SCA World Championship standards. This is the moment we live for.
