Our Story

Born from a
Single Obsession

It started with a second-hand drum roaster, a cramped unit in Tiong Bahru, and the stubborn conviction that Singapore deserved world-class specialty coffee.

Marcus had spent a decade travelling origin farms and working in roasteries across Melbourne, Tokyo, and Copenhagen. When he came home, he brought everything he'd learned — and built something he couldn't find anywhere else.

Seven years on, Noir Brew Co. is three locations, twelve farm partnerships, and over 12,000 cups a month. But the obsession hasn't changed. The only thing that matters is what's in the cup.

The Journey
2018
The Founding

Marcus Chen opens a 60 sqft roastery in Tiong Bahru with a second-hand drum roaster purchased from Melbourne. The first espresso shot is pulled at 5:47am.

2019
First Direct Trade

We fly to Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia. After two weeks on the farm, we sign Noir Brew's first direct-trade agreement. The Ethiopian Yirgacheffe becomes our first origin offering.

2019
Singapore Specialty Coffee Championship — 1st Place

Marcus enters the SSCC roasting division with our Ethiopian Natural profile. Takes gold on the first attempt. The industry takes notice.

2020
Dempsey Hill Opens

Our second location launches in Dempsey Hill — a 1,200 sqft café with a visible roasting floor. Guests can watch beans transform live.

2021
Bean Club Launches

We launch our direct-to-door bean subscription. 500 subscribers sign up in the first month. We expand sourcing to Colombia, Guatemala, Kenya, and Indonesia.

2022
Jewel Changi Arrival

Noir Brew lands in Jewel Changi Airport — our most ambitious design yet. We serve over 1,000 cups on opening weekend.

2024
SCA Roastery of the Year

The Specialty Coffee Association names Noir Brew Co. Southeast Asia's Roastery of the Year. We pledge 1% of revenue to carbon offset programs.

What Drives Us

Four Values.
No Compromise.

These aren't framed posters on a wall. They're the decisions we make every single morning before the first shot is pulled.

🌿
Radical Transparency

Every bag tells you exactly which farm, which farmer, and what we paid. No vague "ethically sourced" labels. Real names. Real numbers.

🔬
Scientific Precision

Every roast profile is logged, tasted, and scored. We iterate until the cup says what the bean wants to say. Feeling is not a process. Data is.

🤝
Long-Term Partnerships

We don't cherry-pick farms season to season. We invest in relationships over years. Our farmers know they have a committed buyer — and that changes everything.

🌍
Genuine Sustainability

200kg of spent grounds composted monthly. Recyclable packaging since day one. 1% of revenue into verified carbon offset programs. Not perfect, but honest.
